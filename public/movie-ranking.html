My top 3 movies of all-time.

Sita Ramam

This is my favourite because of the beautiful love story.

3 Idiots

It shows us what are the different challenges faced by engineering students and what a true friendship can put a positive impact in your life.

Taare Zameen Par

It shows a beautiful relationship and a friend like bond between a teacher and a student,the spirit of never giving up on the student by the teacher is amazing to watch.
